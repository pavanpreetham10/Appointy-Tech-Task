IPL should ban Kohli and ABD, jokes KL Rahul

During an interaction on Instagram, RCB captain Virat Kohli asked KXIP captain KL Rahul about one T20 rule he'd like to change. Responding jokingly, Rahul said, "I'd ask IPL to ban you and AB de Villiers." He added, "Once you achieve...certain amount of runs [like 5,000 IPL runs], people should say this is enough...Now, you guys let other guys [score]."
